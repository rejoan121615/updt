Our Founder's

Manifesto

Hi, I'm Jeremy, CEO here at unipoint

I was born in California and grew up in a small town in Arkansas.

My first encounter with computers was a commodore 64. I instantly fell in love with it and quickly found myself obsessively reading manuals and learning all I could.

Years later, while getting my computer science degree, I noticed that IT experts are notorious for being technically arrogant.

I quickly decided that our mission here at unipoint should be to go against this pattern.

We're always looking to build long-lasting relationships with all of our clients. It's very important for us to have genuine interest and care for IT problems. We strive to make every interaction respectful, easy, and comfortable.

Advanced technology does not exist in a vacuum, it is connected to real people, real businesses. You are hiring experts who use technology to help solve real business problems, without talking down to you.

It may sound cliche, but my thirst for knowledge and passion for technology drives unipoint. It’s our duty and responsibility to stay ahead of the curve for all our clients.

IT should not be a burden. It’s simply a wise way to enable future innovation and stability for your business.
